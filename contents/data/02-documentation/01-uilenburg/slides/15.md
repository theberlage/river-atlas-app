# Residential Spaces Today
In the 1990s, canal houses on Oudeschans 5-11 were merged to create a new apartment complex with a complete interior renovation, keeping only the facade as a remnant of the past. The narrow building on Oudeschans 13 was renovated based on the existing structure to a 4-story modern home. Boats that are tied to the east side of Oudeschans also became homes with addresses and postal codes.
